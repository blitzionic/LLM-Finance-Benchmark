Question:

The Litzenberger Company has projected the following quarterly sales amounts for the coming year:

Q1 $760

Q2 $790

Q3 $870

Q4 $950

a. Accounts receivable at the beginning of the year at $350. Litzenberger has a 45-day collection period. Calculate cash collections in each of the four quarters. (Do not round intermediate calculations and round your answers to 2 decimal places.)

b. Recalculate the cash collections with a collection period of 60 days. (Do not round intermediate calculations and round your answers to 2 decimal places.)

c. Recalculate the cash collections with a collection period of 30 days. (Do not round intermediate calculations and round your answers to 2 decimal places.)

Sale

A transaction is known as sales where two parties are involved in which the seller handovers the services/goods to the buyer and receives the value of the goods/service. In such a transaction, both the parties satisfies their individual needs in terms of money and goods.

a. Calculate cash collections in each of the four quarters

Ending account receivable$380$395$435$475

45-day collection period

Cash will be collected Opening receivable plus half of current quarter sale.
