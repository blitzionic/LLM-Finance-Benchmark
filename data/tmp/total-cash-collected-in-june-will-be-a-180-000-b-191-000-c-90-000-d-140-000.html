Business Finance Cash management

Total cash collected in June will be a. $180,000 b. $191,000 c. $90,000 d. $140,000

Question:

Budgeted sales for the second quarter of the current year are as follows:

Budgeted Sales
April$150,000
May200,000
June180,000

The company collects 20% in the month of sale, 70% in the first month following the sale and 10% in the second month following the sale.

Total cash collected in June will be

a. $180,000

b. $191,000

c. $90,000

d. $140,000

Cash collection:

Cash collection is a part of account receivables which is converted into cash. It can be said as the payment made by the person to whom the invoice is issued. Every company has different terms and condition for making collection.

Answer and Explanation:


Below table shows the calculation:

Particulars April May June
20% in month of sale $30,000 $40,000 $36,000
70% in month following the sale 0 $105,000 $140,000
10% in second month following the sale 0 0 $15,000
Total $30,000 $145,000 $191,000


Total cash collected in June will be $191,000.
