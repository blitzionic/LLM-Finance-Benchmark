Cash disbursements would not include payments for: a. accounts receivable b. dividends c. income...

Question:

Cash disbursements would not include payments for:

a. accounts receivable

b. dividends

c. income taxes

d. marketing and administrative expenses

e. capital budget expenditures

Golden Rules of Accounts:

The personal account rule requires debiting the receiver and crediting the giver. The real account rule requires debiting what comes in and crediting what goes out of the business. The nominal account rule requires debiting all expenses and losses and creating all incomes and gains.

Answer and Explanation: 1


Correct Answer: Option a. accounts receivable.

Explanation:

Cash disbursements account for all the transactions that result in an outflow of cash. Accounts receivable represent the revenue that is earned but not yet received and it is a source of cash inflow. Hence cash disbursements would not include payments for accounts receivable.
