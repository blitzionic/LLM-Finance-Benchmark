Question:

By getting closer to the source of payment, lockboxes can be used to reduce:

A. availability or clearing float.

B. mail float.

C. in-house processing float.

D. disbursement float.

E. None of the above.

Cash Concentration:

An electronic funds transfer that is used to transfer funds among the business accounts is called cash concentration and disbursement. It was developed by the National Automated Clearing House Association. The disbursement clear overnight under this type of electronic funds transfer and a lot of time and interest are saved as a result.

Answer and Explanation: 1


Correct answer: Option B) mail float.

Explanation:

The time required for a check payment to reach the payee from the payer via the postal system is called mail float. A lockbox system is a mailing address that is operated by the bank to which a company directs its customers to send the invoice amounts charged to them. Hence, lockboxes can be used to reduce the mail float by getting closer to the source of payment.
